Instructions
Instructions:
  • Press Start to begin the game.
  • Select a level, which will determine the sequence length - ranging from 2 to 7 tones.
  • A unique sequence based on your chosen level will be played. Memorize it!
  • After the sequence is played, you'll have 10 seconds to recreate it accurately.
  • Correctly recreating the sequence earns you points, proportional to the chosen level.
  • If you recreate the sequence incorrectly, miss a beat, or run out of time, you'll lose 1 life.
  • Keep progressing through rounds with new sequences to challenge your memory and rhythm skills.
